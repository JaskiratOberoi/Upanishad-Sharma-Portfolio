About

Upanishad Sharma writes about technology the way readers actually experience it — as something you play, argue about, and live with.

He has spent five years turning fast-moving beats into readable journalism: breaking coverage of OpenAI's leadership crisis, hands-on hardware reviews, industry analysis, and opinion writing with a personal voice. As an editor, he pairs that reporting instinct with data-driven strategy — SEO, audience growth, and mentoring writers — having grown Beebom's gaming section into the most-viewed vertical on the site.

He holds a Master's in Multimedia Journalism from the University of Westminster, London, and is currently freelancing while pursuing full-time editorial and reporting roles.
